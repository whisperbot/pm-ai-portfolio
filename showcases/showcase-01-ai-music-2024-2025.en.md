# Showcase 01 | AI Music (2024-2025)

> Role: Product Manager (PM)
> 
> Keywords: AI-native app, music consumption, LLM + retrieval/recommendation, growth, experiment-driven iteration

## 1) Background
Under the vision of an "AI personal assistant", music was identified as a high-value domain:
- content consumption + companionship value,
- explicit search intents + personalized recommendation needs,
- a representative scenario for building AI-native application capabilities.

## 2) Goals
- **Capability goal**: reach top-tier user experience in music scenarios.
- **Business goal**: become a key vertical that contributes to long-term retention and engagement.
- **Mechanism goal**: establish a reusable iteration framework (evaluation + experiments + sampling checks).

## 3) Key Responsibilities
1. Built an iteration loop of **test → evaluation → experiment → sampling checks**.
2. Drove the strategy of **LLM strengths + traditional retrieval/recommendation strengths**.
3. Turned user insights (query clustering, segmentation, behavior paths) into product decisions.
4. Supported architecture evolution from early multi-agent patterns to more unified model-tool collaboration.

## 4) Reusable Product Methods
### 4.1 Evaluation + Experiment as Dual Engines
- In open-domain conversational products, intuition alone is not enough.
- Use offline evaluation + online experiments to continuously validate decisions.

### 4.2 LLM + Traditional Retrieval/Recommendation Collaboration
- Intent-specific paths for exact search, similar recommendation, scenario recommendation, and exploratory listening.
- LLM improves understanding/generation; traditional systems ensure relevance, personalization, and controllability.

### 4.3 User Understanding System
- Continuous profiling, behavior analysis, and query clustering.
- Split analysis by intent types (search vs. consumption) to avoid “average metric blindness”.
- Optimize entry paths and expectation alignment for new users.

## 5) Outcomes (from internal retrospective)
- Built a sustainable iteration mechanism and quality baseline for the music vertical.
- Observed positive signals across long-term metrics, activity, and listening duration in multiple rounds of experiments.
- Validated music as a high-value module in AI assistant experiences.

> Note: This is a public, desensitized summary focusing on reusable methods and directional outcomes.

## 6) Key Learnings
- **Mechanism before features** in high-uncertainty domains.
- **Segment before aggregate** for reliable decision-making.
- **Collaborate before replace** between LLM and traditional systems.
